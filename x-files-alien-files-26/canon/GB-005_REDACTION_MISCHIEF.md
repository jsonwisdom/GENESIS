## Story beat

Baby Gray discovers that the redaction stamp is not merely an instrument of secrecy but a toy, a shield, and a tiny act of resistance. Seated inside an overfilled records office, Baby Gray gleefully stamps selected lines while exhausted analysts fail to notice that the most revealing sentence remains untouched.

## Theme

Redaction as bureaucracy, concealment, and mischievous agency.

## Expression

`mischievous`

## Expression justification

The Character Bible defines shocked, surprised, or bewildered as the default. This entry intentionally uses a mischievous expression because the Redaction theme depends on Baby Gray understanding the system well enough to play with it. The deviation is local to GB-005 and does not alter GB-003 or GB-004.
