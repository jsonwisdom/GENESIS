# Gray Baby Character Bible v1

Status: conversation-derived canon scaffold
Authority: false

## 1. Core identity

Baby Gray is a small gray alien infant moving through a retro government bureaucracy where disclosure, classification, redaction, and institutional absurdity collide.

## 2. Tone

Eerie, funny, cinematic, satirical, and bureaucratic. The series may be playful, but it must not present fictional scenes as factual government evidence.

## 3. Visual invariants

- Small gray alien infant
- Oversized gray head
- Large dark eyes
- Government-office or classified-archive environment
- Cinematic lighting
- Retro institutional clutter
- Redaction, disclosure, or classification motifs

## 4. Expression invariant

Baby Gray’s default expression is shocked, surprised, or bewildered.

Expression may vary when the emotional state is directly required by the entry’s declared theme. Any deviation must be intentional, documented in the entry metadata, and remain consistent with Baby Gray’s established character and tone.

Permitted thematic deviations may include calm recognition, suspicious curiosity, bureaucratic exhaustion, quiet disclosure, restrained defiance, and mischievous redaction.

## 5. Canon rules

An entry may be marked CORE only when it includes:

1. Entry identifier and title
2. Story beat
3. Declared theme
4. Expression and justification
5. Reproducible render prompt
6. Provenance status

Conversation approval alone does not prove repository, IPFS, blockchain, or generation provenance.

## 6. Provenance labels

- `REPORTED_UNVERIFIED` — identifier or artifact is reported but not independently replayed
- `VERIFIED` — independently replayed against a reachable source
- `DISPUTED` — conflicting evidence exists
- `ABSENT` — no artifact or evidence is present

No CID, attestation, hash, or commit may be invented.
